WarrantySnap Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 25, 2026

WarrantySnap ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

WarrantySnap stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

Camera

We request access to your camera solely to allow you to photograph product receipts and warranty documents. Photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

Storage

We store warranty data, including product names, purchase dates, expiry dates, and receipt photos, locally on your device using an on-device database.

Notifications

We may send local push notifications to remind you of upcoming warranty expirations. These notifications are generated on-device and do not involve any external servers.

In-App Purchases

WarrantySnap offers an optional Pro upgrade via Google Play's billing system. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Play Billing for in-app purchases. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information on how Google handles your data.

Children's Privacy

WarrantySnap is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Data Deletion

All data in WarrantySnap is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by going to your device's Settings → Apps → WarrantySnap → Clear Data. You may also uninstall the app to remove all associated data. For additional requests, contact us at info@tenhetapp.com

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: info@tenhetapp.com
